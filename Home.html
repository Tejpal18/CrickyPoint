About Crickypoint

Cricket is First Love

Our Academies are an extension of our own love for sport and we’re driven to deliver courses that we would want to attend. The whole team here at HQ are active sports people who have a real passion for developing athletes at a grassroots level, regardless of if they are just getting into the sport or if they want to take the next step in their development. We believe in inspiring young Athletes and therefore ensure our Star Coach or Player attends for a minimum of a full day and coaches every group. We also ensure there is time for a Q&A to hear how they got into their sport and to find out what it’s like to participate at the highest level.
